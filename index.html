Bugatti Chiron

One of the most iconic hypercars in the world. Luxury Craftsmanship: High-grade leather, polished aluminum, and carbon fiber throughout the cabin.Top Speed: Electronically limited to 261 mph (420 km/h). The Chiron Super Sport 300+ variant can exceed 304 mph.

Porsche 911 Cabriolet

A luxury sports car produced by Porsche. Luxurious seats, often customizable with leather and contrast stitching. Turbocharged flat-six engines with varying power outputs, ranging from ~379 hp (911 Carrera) to ~640 hp (911 Turbo S). Base Carrera Cabriolet: Starts at ~$120,000.

Tesla Roadster

A high-performance electric sports car designed by Tesla, Inc. Acceleration: Claimed 0-60 mph in just 1.9 seconds. Top Speed: Claimed 650 mph. The base model of the second-generation Tesla Roadster is priced at $200,000.

1955 Dodge Royal Lancer

A classic American full-size sedan from Dodge's mid-century lineup,Known for its striking two-tone paint schemes, prominent chrome detailing, and jet-age-inspired styling. Offered with a "Red Ram" V8 engine, which was Dodge's early HEMI engine, providing robust performance for its time. Classic Car Market Value: Prices can range from $15,000 to over $50,000 depending on the condition, originality, and restorations
